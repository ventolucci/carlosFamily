about me

Denis, who works as a product recall specialist for an unnamed car company. Because of the stress of his job and the jet lag brought upon by frequent business trips, he begins to suffer from recurring insomnia. When he seeks treatment, his doctor advises him to start coding to "see what real suffering is like". He finds that coding the problems of others alleviates his insomnia. Denis unique treatment works until he meets Marla Singer, another "tourist" who does coding. The possibly disturbed Marla reminds the Denis that he is a faker who does not belong there. He begins to hate Marla for keeping him from coding, and, therefore, from sleeping. After a confrontation, the two agree to code separate to avoid each other. The truce is uneasy, and the Denis insomnia returns.
